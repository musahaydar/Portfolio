About

Hello! I'm Musa. I'm currently a master's student studying computer science at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. I graduated with a B.S. in Computer Science and a minor in music from the University of Michigan in 2022.

I'm interested in building and testing systems. The coursework I've taken includes distributed systems, computer networks, compilers, and programming languages. During 2021, I participated in research in testing persistent memory systems for crash consistency bugs. Currently, I'm conducting research on symbolic execution.

I'm a graduate student instructor for EECS 370: Introduction to Computer Organization. Topics in this course include assembly language programming, compilation and linking, and CPU architecture.

I'm also a game developer, pixel artist, and composer. During 2021, I was the president of WolverineSoft, the game development student organization at the University of Michigan. You can see some of the games I've made at game jams here and some of the music I've composed here.
