The Broken Mansion

The Broken Mansion is an exploration puzzle game created in 48 hours for the 2020 Global Game Jam. It's one of my favorite games I've made so far.

I had created the 3D style a short while before the global game jam, and decided I wanted to try making something with it. I spent the first day of the jam implementing the billboarding sprites and edge detection systems. I came up with the compass system later in the jam, while walking to the site early in the morning. The result was an atmospheric exploration game that I'm very proud of.
