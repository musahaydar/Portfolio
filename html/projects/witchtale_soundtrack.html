Witchtale Soundtrack

As part of the video game music course at UMich, I had the opportunity to compose music for a team in EECS 494, the game development course. I worked with the team creating Witchtale, a retro-inspired platformer game. To match the aesthetic, I composed four tracks of chiptune-inspired music.
